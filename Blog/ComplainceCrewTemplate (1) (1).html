Introduction

In today’s digital banking landscape, ensuring compliance with banking regulations is crucial for financial institutions. Compliance Crew AI (CCAI) is an AI-powered governance solution designed to address the critical challenges of data governance and risk management in the banking sector. Non-compliance with industry regulations like PCI DSS and GDPR can result in severe consequences, including regulatory fines, data breaches, and financial fraud. CCAI aims to mitigate these risks by implementing a robust, AI-driven governance framework tailored for banking compliance.

Prototype Overview

Enter Compliance Crew Utility, AI-powered ally in automating compliance

How It Works

Ensuring regulatory compliance can be complex, but with our Compliance Crew Utility, we streamline the process using AI-driven automation.

Step-by-Step Process:

Prototype Demo: AI-Powered Compliance in Action

1️. Metadata Extraction & Scanning

    The system scans banking metadata from IDMC (MCC/CDGC/CDQ) to identify and extract key data elements relevant to compliance.

2. AI-Driven Compliance Insights

  • Users interact with an AI-powered chatbot (Gemini AI) to ask regulatory compliance-related questions.
  • AI provides automated recommendations for classification, glossary terms, and data quality rules, reducing manual effort.

3. Automated Data Classification & Governance Framework

  • AI assigns predefined classification labels based on banking regulatory compliance requirements (PCI DSS, GDPR).
  • System automatically generates structured CDGC asset templates, including:
    • Policy
    • Regulation
    • Business Term
    • Metric
    • Data Quality Template

4. Data Quality & Risk Evaluation

  • AI maps relevant Data Quality Rules to classified data elements, ensuring accuracy and compliance.
  • The system calculates Data Quality Scores to measure compliance adherence and identify potential risks.

5. Final Compliance Validation

  • The enriched metadata is uploaded back into IDMC for validation.
  • The system re-scans resources to confirm classification accuracy and adherence to governance policies.
